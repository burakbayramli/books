Base classes for curves in 2D (WmlCurve2), either single components (WmlSingleCurve2) or defined piecewise (WmlMultipleCurve2). Includes support for computing position, derivatives, tangents, normals, speed, arc length, curvature, reparameterization by arc length (given arc length, find curve parameter), and various subdivision methods:
WmlCurve2.h , WmlCurve2.cpp , WmlSingleCurve2.h , WmlSingleCurve2.cpp , WmlMultipleCurve2.h , WmlMultipleCurve2.cpp
