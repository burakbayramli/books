Gelatin Blob (icosahedron):
GelatinBlob.h , GelatinBlob.cpp , PhysicsModule.h , PhysicsModule.cpp , GelatinBlob.mpg

A gelatinous blob that is oscillating due to small, random forces. This blob has the masses located at the vertices of an icosahedron with additional masses of infinite weight to help stabilize the oscillations. The springs connecting the blob to the infinite masses are shown in white.
