Specular Highlights After Texture is Drawn:
SpecularAfterTexture.h , SpecularAfterTexture.cpp

The program displays the biped with an environment map. The specular lighting is drawn so that the environment texture does not tint it. Press the 'L' key to turn off the effect. The specular effect is still there, but the specular highlights are tinted by the texture. The 'L' key is a toggle to turn on/off the effect.
