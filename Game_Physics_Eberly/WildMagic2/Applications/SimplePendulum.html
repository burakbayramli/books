Simple Pendulum (numerical experiments):
SimplePendulum.cpp

A simple pendulum whose mass is affected by gravity. No frictional forces are assumed at the joint. The pairs of images below show the angle of the pendulum, measured from the vertical, and the region of stability for choosing the step size of the numerical solver. The Game Physics book goes into detail about how these pictures are generated.
