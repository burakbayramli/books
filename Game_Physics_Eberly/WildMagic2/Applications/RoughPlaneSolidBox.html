Particle on a Plane With Friction:
RoughPlaneSolidBox.h , RoughPlaneSolidBox.cpp , PhysicsModule.h , PhysicsModule.cpp

A solid box moves on a planar surface that is slanted relative to the xy-plane. The box is affected by gravitational force. The surface exerts a frictional force on the bottom of the box. The screen shots show the box at various times in the simulation.
