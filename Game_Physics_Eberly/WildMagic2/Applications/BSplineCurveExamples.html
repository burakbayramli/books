B-Spline Curve Examples:
BSplineCurveExamples.h , BSplineCurveExamples.cpp

Six pairs of B--spline curves of various types. The right image of each pair shows the deformed curve by modifying one control point. Upper left: open, uniform, not closed. Upper right: open, uniform, closed. Middle left: open, nonuniform, closed. Middle right: open, nonuniform, closed. Lower left: periodic, not closed. Lower right: periodic, closed.
