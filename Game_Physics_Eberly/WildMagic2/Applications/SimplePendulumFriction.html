Simple Pendulum, Joint has Friction:
SimplePendulumFriction.h , SimplePendulumFriction.cpp , PhysicsModule.h , PhysicsModule.cpp , SimplePendulumFriction.mpg

The simple pendulum where the joint has friction. The source code allows you to overdamp the joint. The pendulum slows down to vertical position with no oscillation about the vertical. You can also underdamp the problem so that the oscillations are few (large friction) or so that the oscillations are many (small friction).
