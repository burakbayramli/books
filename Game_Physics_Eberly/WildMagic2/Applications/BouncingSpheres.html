Bouncing Spheres (rigid body dynamics):
BouncingSpheres.h , BouncingSpheres.cpp , RigidBall.h , RigidBall.cpp , BouncingSpheres.mpg

A collection of spheres of various sizes bounce around in a room. The front most wall of the room is invisible, so when you see balls bouncing against, that behavior is expected. Angular momentum is not taken into account, so the spheres do not spin while bouncing.
