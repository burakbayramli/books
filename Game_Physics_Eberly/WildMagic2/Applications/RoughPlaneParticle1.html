Particle on a Plane With Friction:
RoughPlaneParticle1.h , RoughPlaneParticle1.cpp , PhysicsModule.h , PhysicsModule.cpp

A particle moves on a planar surface that is slanted relative to the xy-plane. The particle is affected by gravitational force. The surface exerts a frictional force on the particle. The screen shot shows the path of the particle, which was given an upward initial velocity.
