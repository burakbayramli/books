Cloth Flapping in a Breeze:
Cloth.h , Cloth.cpp , PhysicsModule.h , PhysicsModule.cpp , Cloth.mpg

A cloth is modeled as a rectangular array of springs. Wind forces make the cloth flap about. Notice that the cloth in the bottom image is stretched in the vertical direction. The stretching occurs while the gravitational and spring forces balance out in the vertical direction during the initial portion of the simulation.
