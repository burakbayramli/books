Refraction and Reflectance:
Refraction.h , Refraction.cpp , Refraction.cg

The top image has only refraction effects. The bottom image has refraction and some Fresnel reflectance. Notice that rendering using both refraction and reflection appears to be more realistic than with refraction alone; the model has a glassier look to it. The texture image is a modification of one that is available from the Cg texture library at http://oss.ckk.chalmers.se/textures/.
