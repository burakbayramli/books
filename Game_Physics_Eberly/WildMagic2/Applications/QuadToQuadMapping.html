Perspective or biquadratic mappings between squares and quadrilaterals:

QuadToQuadMapping.cpp , Perspective Mapping Between Quadrilaterals ,
 Perspective Mapping Between Two Convex Quadrilaterals

The top left image is a perspective mapping of a square to a quadrilateral. The top right image is a biquadratic mapping of the same square to the same quadrilateral. Notice that the perspective mapping preserves lines, but the biquadratic mapping distorts lines. The bottom left image is a perspective mapping of a quadrilateral to a square. The bottom right image is a biquadratic mapping of the same quadrilateral to the same square. Again, the perspective mapping preserves lines, but the biquadratic mapping distorts them.
