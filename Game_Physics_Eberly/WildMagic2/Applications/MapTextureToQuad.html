 Perspective Mapping Between Two Convex Quadrilaterals

The application allows you to interactively change the convex quadrilateral on which a texture is mapped by dragging its vertices with the mouse. The left image is the original texture on a rectangle. The right image is obtained by dragging corners of the rectangle.
