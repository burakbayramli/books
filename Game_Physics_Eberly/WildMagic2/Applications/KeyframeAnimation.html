Simple application to show TCB keyframe animation:

KeyframeAnimation.h , KeyframeAnimation.cpp , PosKey.h , PosKey.inl , PosKey.cpp , PosSpline.h , PosSpline.cpp , RotKey.h , RotKey.inl , RotKey.cpp , RotSpline.h , RotSpline.cpp , Key Frame Interpolation via Splines and Quaternions

Although the splines are built for 3D, the application shows only a 2D animation for illustrative purposes. The animated object is a stick-figure leg. The important features are how to preprocess the position and rotation keys to set up the TCB splines. The position spline also shows how to compute positions by specifying arc length. This allows selection of points on the curve that are uniformly spaced with respect to arc length, thereby allowing constant speed motion of an object along the curve. The key frames are drawn in black. The interpolated object is drawn in red. The time increases from left to right, then top to bottom. Do notice that the leg joints are rotating as well as the leg translating.
