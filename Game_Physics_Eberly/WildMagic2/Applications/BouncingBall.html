Bouncing Ball:
BouncingBall.h , BouncingBall.cpp , DeformableBall.h , DeformableBall.inl , DeformableBall.cpp , BouncingBall.mpg

A deformable body initially in the shape of a sphere is bounced on a floor. When the body hits the floor, it starts to deform. At the instant of maximum deformation, the body bounces off the floor and gradually returns to its spherical shape. The top image shows the bouncing ball with only the implicit surface deformation. The bottom image shows an additional deformation of nonuniform scaling by applying an affine transformation.
