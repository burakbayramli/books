Test for overlap of intervals (1D), axis-aligned rectangles (2D), and axis-aligned boxes (3D). The initial overlaps are computed using a sort-and-sweep algorithm. As the objects are moved, the incremental updates of the overlap status are performed using an insertion sort. This takes advantage of spatial and temporal coherence. On small motions, the objects are nearly sorted, so the insertion sort is very fast.
